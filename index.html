About Us

Tea isn’t just a drink—it’s a moment, a vibe, a reset button for life’s chaos. At Da Bomb Tea Shop, we craft seasonal tea blends, aka Bombs, that energize the body and soothe the soul. Whether it’s a chilled Sunshine Boost or a warm and cozy Flu Bomb, there’s a Bomb for every season and mood.

Our mission: For every cup you buy, we donate one to someone in need. Because everyone deserves their own tea moment—whether it’s in a cozy kitchen or on a city sidewalk.
